UX-design

Patterns of daily occupation

This project was done during the course “Designstudio Health and smart societies”. Our client was two professors from Lund university. The brief was to create a concept for an application that could track and log a user’s time, place and everyday activities. The application would be used as a complement for individuals who are in need of occupational therapy.

Project participants: Emma Bengtsson, Jakob Wisborg, Jurgen Hasuka, Natalie Janzen.
